Pastel de Choclo Recipe

A finished Pastel de Choclo dish

This is a typical Chilean dish, but is also eaten in Argentina and Peru with some variations in the recipe, sometimes using cornmeal instead of freshly mashed sweet corn. Serve with Chilean salad and/or pebre.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ paprika
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • ½ broiled chicken, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons raisins, or to taste
  • 1 ½ tablespoons chopped Kalamanta olives, or to taste
